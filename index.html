Behind every injury is a person trying to find their way back — to their family, their routine, their sense of self.

That's what drives me. For over 20 years, I've walked alongside people navigating some of the most difficult moments of their lives — after workplace injuries, motor vehicle accidents, and through the challenges of disability and ageing. I don't just help people return to work. I help them come back to life.

I believe recovery is deeply personal. Every person I work with has a unique story, and I take time to truly understand what matters to them — what they've lost, what they're hoping for, and what it would mean to get there. I bring that same care into every conversation with families, healthcare providers, case managers, and insurers.
